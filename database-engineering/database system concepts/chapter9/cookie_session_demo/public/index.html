About Cookies & Sessions

Cookies

Small pieces of data stored on your device by the web browser. They are used to remember stateful information (like items in a shopping cart) or to record the user's browsing activity.

Sessions

A way to store data on the server-side to be used across multiple pages. A session ID is usually stored in a cookie on the client-side to link the user to their session data on the server.

How to Check Cookies

You can inspect the cookies stored by this website using your browser's developer tools:

  • Chrome/Edge: Right-click > Inspect > Application tab > Storage > Cookies
  • Firefox: Right-click > Inspect > Storage tab > Cookies
  • Safari: Right-click > Inspect Element > Storage tab > Cookies

Look for a cookie named connect.sid (or similar) after logging in.
